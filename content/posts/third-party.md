Third Party APIs, aka _libraries_ or _frameworks_ are essential in software, embedded maybe not as much.
Modern software is extremely complex and even simple functionality that we take for granted is quite difficult.

Authentication and Authorization, Data storage and retrieval, Dependency Injection, Building, Package Management, Rendering,
Logging, Error handling etc are often implemented with third party tool because it isn't worth the time, money and risk to do it one's self.
