Protocols are an essential concept is software. We have HTTP, TCP, UDP and many more protocols.

Interfaces define the connection point between two systems so they can interact.
Protocols define the rules for communication between two systems.

For example, a traffic light is an interface. Red, green and yellow have meaning.
